# SalleQ Demo Script

1. Open the Databricks App home screen.
2. In patient mode, accept the non-emergency consent and optionally allow location.
3. Enter a low-acuity symptom such as `Minor ankle sprain after stepping off a curb 3 hours ago`.
4. Review the provisional CTAS cards and recommendation table.
5. Reserve a queue slot and show queue tracking.
6. Switch to staff mode.
7. Refresh the staff dashboard and open the top facility.
8. Ask the assistant: `What is the next 2-hour arrival load for my site?`
9. Show the clinical safety note and explain that CTAS is provisional routing support only.
